Mini Meatloaves

1 - egg
3/4 cup - milk
1 cup - shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 cup - quick cooking oats
1 tsp - salt
1 pound - ground beef
2/3 cup - ketchup
1/4 cup - packed brown sugar
1 1/2 tsp - prepared mustard

Make Like So

Preheat over to 350°F.
In a large bowl, combine egg, milk, cheese, oats and salt.
Add ground beef, mix well, and form mixture into 8 mini meatloaves.
Lightly grease a baking dish, and place meatloaves.
In a separate bowl, combine ketchup, brown sugar and mustard. Stir thoroughly, spread over each meatloaf.
Bake, uncovered, for 45 minutes.

Meatballs

1 pound - ground beef
1/2 cup - Italian style bread crumbs
1/4 cup - milk
1/2 tsp - salt
1/2 tsp - worcestershire sauce
1/4 tsp - pepper
1/4 cup - onion (finely chopped)
1 - egg

Make Like So

Preheat oven to 400°F.
In a large bowl, mix bread crumbs, salt, worcestershire sauce, pepper, onions and milk.
Add ground beef, mix.
Shape into 20 to 24 1.5 inch meatballs. Place 1 inch apart on pan.
Bake uncovered for 18 to 22 minutes, or until no longer pink in the center.
