Banana Pineapple Muffins

1/2 cup - canola oil
3/4 cup - white sugar
1 - egg
1/2 cup - crushed pineapple
2 - bananas (mashed)
1 tsp - vanilla
11/2 cup - flour
1 tsp - baking soda
1/2 tsp - baking powder
1/2 tsp - cinnamon
1/4 tsp - nutmeg

Make Like So

Whisk together canola oil, sugar and egg.
Add in Bananas, pineapple and vanilla, mix well.
Add Flour, baking soda, baking powder, cinnamon and nutmeg until just mixed.
Barely fill the muffin liners half full.
-Muffins will rise VERY high-
Bake at 350°F for 15 - 20 minutes depending on your oven
-Muffins will get quite brown on top-
