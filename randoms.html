Mayonnaise

(Store in fridge for up to 1 week)

2 egg yolks
1 heaped tsp - Dijon mustard
500ml - oil
1-2 tbsp - white wine vinegar
splash of fresh lemon juice
Sea salt to taste

Make Like So

Whisk egg yolks in a bowl.
Add mustard and whisk together.
Gradually add about half the oil, very slowly at first, whisking continuously for
3-5 minutes, or until thickened.
After adding about half the oil, whisky in 1 Tbsp of vinegar - this will loosen mixture, and give it a paler colour.
Continue gradually adding the remaining oil, whisking continuously.
Season with a pinch of salt, a squeeze of lemon juice and a little more vinegar (if needed.)
