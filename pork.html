Egg Rolls

Egg/Spring roll wrappers
½ cup of thinly julienned carrots
½ cup thinly julienned red peppers
1 cup chopped cabbage
1 tbsp soy sauce
1 tsp sesame oil
½ tsp oyster sauce
2 tsp sugar
¼ lb. ground pork
3 tbsp scallions
1 tsp ginger
1 tbsp garlic

Make Like So

Heat oil in wok and add scallions, ginger and garlic. Allow to cook down.
Add peppers, carrots and cabbage. Stir until wilted.
Push all the ingredients to one side of the wok, add oil and pork, cook until browned.
Mix, add soy sauce, sesame oil and sugar.
Stir and remove.
Moisten edges of wrap with egg wash.
Add 2 Tbsp of filling to middle of wrap, on a diagonal.
Wrap tight, starting with corner closest.
Fry in oil until golden brown on all sides.
